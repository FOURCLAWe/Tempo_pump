Tempo Pump

Rules

1. Creation
Connect your wallet before creating a token. Submitting the form deploys a new launch project on-chain.
2. Trading
All trades are settled in USDC. Buy and sell actions follow the live on-chain pricing curve, and page data is read directly from chain state.
3. Ranking
Token lists can be sorted by creation order or market cap. Market cap is calculated from live on-chain price and total supply.
4. Ticker
The top ticker only shows real on-chain trades. If no live events are available, the site shows an empty live-trade state instead of fake activity.
